Privacy Policy for Aluna – Period Tracker

Effective date: August 7, 2025

Aluna (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

  • Menstrual cycle entries (dates, symptoms), ovulation/fertility information
  • Language preference
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Non-personal usage analytics (aggregate)

We do not collect your name, email address, or other directly identifying information unless you explicitly choose to share it.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide predictions and core functionality
  • Improve app quality and user experience
  • Generate anonymized usage insights

3. Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties unless required by law or you explicitly consent.

4. Third Parties

We do not include advertising or third-party trackers. If this changes, we will update this Policy and inform you where required.

5. Gmail and Email Services

Aluna does not use Gmail, email, or any other external communication services. The app operates entirely offline with your data stored locally on your device.

6. Your Rights

You can review and delete your data at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this link.
