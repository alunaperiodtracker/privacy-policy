Privacy Policy for Aluna – Period Tracker

Effective date: August 7, 2025

Aluna (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed and your choices.

1. Data We Process

  • Menstrual cycle entries you add (dates, symptoms), ovulation/fertility information.
  • Language preference used by the app.

We do not collect your name, email address, or other directly identifying information.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide predictions and core functionality based on the entries you add.
  • Improve the app’s on-device experience (no server processing).

3. Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or third parties, unless required by law or you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., export).

4. Third Parties

The app contains no advertising and no third-party trackers.

5. Email & Connectivity

The app works offline and does not send your entries anywhere.

6. Your Rights

You can review and delete your data at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children.

8. Changes

If we update this Policy, the latest version will be shown inside the app and on the listing page in Google Play.
